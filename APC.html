About APC Explorer

This page displays Article Processing Charges (APCs) data from six large scholarly publishers (Elsevier, Frontiers, MDPI, PLOS, Springer Nature, and Wiley) covering the period 2019–2023.

The data comes from an open dataset by Butler, L.-A., Hare, M., Schönfelder, N., Schares, E., Alperin, J. P., & Haustein, S. (2024). An open dataset of article processing charges from six large scholarly publishers (2019–2023).
arXiv:2406.08356 — Harvard Dataverse

This interactive explorer is part of the Where to Publish? project.
